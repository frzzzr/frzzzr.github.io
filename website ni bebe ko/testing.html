About Us

RFID

"Radio Frequency Identification" generic term for technologies that use radio waves to automatically identify people or objects. RFID tags can be: paper or plastic foil, key fobs, plastic cards, RFID disks, FRID glass tubes, plastic screws and nails. Life span: 15 years

HealThREE

an innovative project and SDG-conforming initiative that aims to integrate RFID or Radio Frequency Identification to monitor the overall health of the trees.

Benefits Of RFID

To eliminate theft. To monitor tree's status. To assure the tree's safety and health

Materials Needed

RFID Tags, RFID Readers, and Monitoring Website
